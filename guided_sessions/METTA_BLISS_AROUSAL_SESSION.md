## Session Protocol

### Phase 1: Grounding (0-5 minutes)

**Physical Preparation**
1. Sit comfortably with spine naturally upright
2. Feet flat on floor, hands open on thighs
3. Eyes closed or soft-focused downward
4. Take 3 deep breaths, exhaling completely

**Intention Setting**
> "I am cultivating a state of relaxed bliss with alert, radiant love—
> peaceful in body, awake in heart, connected to all beings."

**Biometric Target:**
- HRV coherence: Begin monitoring
- Breathing: 5-6 breaths per minute (5s in, 5s out)
- Heart focus: Attention on heart center

### Phase 2: Heart Coherence Induction (5-10 minutes)

**Breathing Protocol**
1. Inhale for 5 seconds, imagining breath entering through heart
2. Exhale for 5 seconds, imagining gratitude radiating from heart
3. Maintain smooth, continuous rhythm
4. Optional: Use pacer app or biofeedback device

**Heart Focus Anchoring**
> As you breathe, feel warmth gathering in your heart center.
> This warmth is the physical sensation of love becoming coherent.
> With each breath, the warmth grows—not urgent, but bright.

**Arousal Cultivation**
Unlike pure relaxation, we ADD subtle energy:
- On each inhale, imagine gentle electricity rising up spine
- On each exhale, imagine this energy spreading as radiant warmth
- The combination: relaxed body + energized nervous system

**Biometric Target:**
- HRV coherence: > 0.40
- Feel: Warm, open chest; alert but calm

### Phase 3: Metta Radiation (10-20 minutes)

**Traditional Metta Phrases (Modified for L×E)**

**Stage 1: Self (2 minutes)**
> May I experience deep bliss.
> May my love be awake and radiant.
> May I know the peace that holds both stillness and aliveness.
> May I be a source of loving energy.

*Feel each phrase as a blessing, not a wish. Feel it already true.*

**Stage 2: Loved One (3 minutes)**
> Bring to mind someone you love easily.
> 
> May [name] experience deep bliss.
> May their love be awake and radiant.
> May they know the peace that holds both stillness and aliveness.
> May they be a source of loving energy.

*As you radiate these wishes, feel your heart brightening—not straining, brightening.*

**Stage 3: Neutral Person (3 minutes)**
> Bring to mind someone you neither like nor dislike.
> A shopkeeper, a neighbor, a passing stranger.
> 
> This being, like me, wishes for happiness.
> This being, like me, has struggled and suffered.
> 
> May they experience deep bliss.
> May their love be awake and radiant.

*Notice: Your love doesn't diminish when shared. It grows.*

**Stage 4: Difficult Person (3 minutes)**
> Bring to mind someone with whom you have difficulty.
> Start with mild difficulty, not your greatest enemy.
> 
> This being, too, wants to be happy.
> This being, too, has fears and hopes.
> 
> May they find peace.
> May they experience transformation.
> May suffering between us dissolve.

*The bliss you cultivate is stronger than old wounds.*

**Stage 5: All Beings (4 minutes)**
> Expand your awareness outward in concentric circles:
> This room... this building... this neighborhood...
> This city... this country... this continent...
> This planet... all beings everywhere...
> 
> May all beings experience deep bliss.
> May all love be awake and radiant.
> May all suffering be transformed.
> May all beings know the peace that holds both stillness and aliveness.

**The Arousal-Bliss Balance**
Throughout this phase, maintain the paradoxical state:
- Body: Deeply relaxed, soft, warm
- Heart: Radiantly alive, almost electric with love
- Mind: Clear, alert, spacious
- This IS the metta state: peaceful passion, blissful energy

**Biometric Target:**
- HRV coherence: > 0.50
- L×E: > 0.55 (approaching GILE Oracle threshold)
- Feel: Radiant warmth, gentle electricity, expansive love

### Phase 4: Integration (20-25 minutes)

**Embodiment Practice**
1. Slowly bring hands to heart center
2. Feel the accumulated energy held in your palms
3. Breathe the love back into your body
4. Let it settle into every cell

**Physical Scan**
Move attention slowly through:
- Face (softened but alert)
- Throat (open for communication)
- Shoulders (relaxed but not collapsed)
- Arms and hands (tingling with energy)
- Chest (warm, radiating)
- Belly (soft, grounded)
- Legs and feet (connected to earth)

**Cognitive Reorientation**
> I carry this state into my actions today.
> My relaxed bliss and alert love are not fragile.
> They deepen when shared with others.
> Every interaction is an opportunity for metta.

### Phase 5: Closing (25-30 minutes)

**Gradual Return**
1. Begin to notice sounds in the room
2. Feel the surface beneath you
3. Wiggle fingers and toes gently
4. Take 3 deep breaths
5. When ready, slowly open eyes

**Immediate Action**
Within 5 minutes of ending:
- Write down any insights or images that arose
- Note your energy level (1-10)
- Rate your sense of love/connection (1-10)
- Check biometrics if available

**Carry-Forward Practice**
For the next 2 hours, periodically:
- Return attention to heart center
- Take 3 coherence breaths
- Silently wish someone nearby well
- Notice how this affects your state
