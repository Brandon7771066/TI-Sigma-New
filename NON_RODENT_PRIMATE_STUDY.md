# Non-Rodent Animal Testing: Primates, Dogs, & Cats
## Extended Species Validation for Human Translation

**Study Type:** Simulated Observational Safety & Efficacy Testing
**Purpose:** Test species closer to humans for better translational validity

---

## Why Non-Rodent Species?

### Limitations of Rodent-Only Studies

| Limitation | Impact | Non-Rodent Solution |
|------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Brain complexity** | Rodents have simpler prefrontal cortex | **Primates** have advanced PFC similar to humans |
| **Social cognition** | Limited in rodents | **Dogs/primates** have complex social behavior |
| **Emotional range** | Rodents show basic emotions | **Primates/dogs** display complex emotional states |
| **Brain size** | 700x smaller than humans | **Primates** are 5-10x closer to human scale |

### Species Selected

1. **Rhesus Macaques** (Macaca mulatta) - Gold standard for neuroscience
2. **Marmosets** (Callithrix jacchus) - Small primates, social, cost-effective
3. **Dogs** (Canis familiaris) - High emotional intelligence, human-bonded
4. **Cats** (Felis catus) - Independent comparison to social species
