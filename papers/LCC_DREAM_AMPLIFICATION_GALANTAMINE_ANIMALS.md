## 1. Galantamine: The Science

### 1.1 Mechanism of Action

Galantamine operates via dual mechanisms:

```
PRIMARY: Acetylcholinesterase (AChE) Inhibition
- Blocks enzyme that breaks down acetylcholine (ACh)
- Increases ACh concentration in synaptic clefts
- Enhances cholinergic transmission

SECONDARY: Nicotinic Receptor Modulation  
- Positive allosteric modulator of nicotinic ACh receptors
- Amplifies ACh effects on cognition
- Unique to galantamine among AChE inhibitors
```

### 1.2 REM Sleep Enhancement

Acetylcholine is the primary neurotransmitter driving REM sleep:

| Effect | Mechanism | Result |
|--------|-----------|--------|
| **Extended REM** | Stabilizes REM-promoting circuits | Longer dream periods |
| **Phasic activity** | Increases REM eye movements | More vivid imagery |
| **Cortical activation** | Frontal lobe engagement | Metacognitive awareness |
| **Memory consolidation** | Enhanced hippocampal ACh | Better dream recall |

### 1.3 Clinical Evidence

**LaBerge et al. (2018) PLOS ONE Study:**
- 121 human participants, double-blind, placebo-controlled
- Wake-Back-To-Bed (WBTB) protocol after 4.5 hours sleep

| Dose | Lucid Dream Rate | Odds Ratio |
|------|------------------|------------|
| Placebo | 14% | 1.0 |
| 4 mg | 27% | 2.29 |
| 8 mg | **42%** | 4.46 |

**Key finding:** 8 mg galantamine increases lucid dreaming 3x over baseline.

## 6. Safety Considerations

### 6.1 Galantamine Side Effects in Animals

```
MILD (common):
- GI upset (nausea, vomiting)
- Decreased appetite
- Lethargy post-administration

MODERATE (uncommon):
- Excessive salivation (cholinergic effect)
- Bradycardia (slowed heart rate)
- Increased urination

SEVERE (rare):
- Seizures (overdose)
- Respiratory depression
- Cardiac arrhythmia

CONTRAINDICATIONS:
- Known cardiac conditions
- GI obstruction
- Pregnancy/nursing
- Combination with other cholinergics
```

### 6.2 Veterinary Supervision Requirements

All animal galantamine protocols require:
1. **Pre-treatment veterinary exam**
2. **Species-appropriate dosing** (not human doses!)
3. **Cardiac monitoring** for first sessions
4. **Emergency protocols** available
5. **Gradual dose escalation** (start low)

### 6.3 Ethical Considerations

```
ETHICAL CHECKLIST:

□ Animal welfare priority over training efficiency
□ No distress indicators during sessions
□ Adequate rest periods between sessions
□ Owner consent and education
□ Veterinary oversight
□ Documentation of all sessions
□ Abort protocols if animal shows negative signs
```
