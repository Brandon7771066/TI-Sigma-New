## Abstract

"The power of intention" is often invoked but rarely operationalized. This paper defines intention power mathematically using the L × E framework, identifies factors that modulate it, categorizes "miracle types" by difficulty, and explains why some individuals achieve more with intention than others.

## Part 1: Defining Intention Power

### 1.1 The LCC Formulation

**Intention Power (IP):**

```
IP = L × E × C × T × D

Where:
L = Love (emotional charge, purity of motive)
E = Existence (physical coherence, bioelectric substrate)
C = Coupling (LCC strength with target)
T = Target Difficulty (inverse - harder targets reduce IP)
D = Duration (time × consistency of intention)
```

### 1.2 Component Definitions

**L (Love) - The Quality of Intention:**
- Selfless intention (for others) > Selfish intention (for self only)
- Aligned with GILE optimization > Misaligned
- Pure, clear, single-pointed > Conflicted, scattered

**E (Existence) - The Physical Substrate:**
- Heart coherence (HRV)
- Brain coherence (EEG)
- Biofield coherence (GDV)
- Physical health

**C (Coupling) - The Connection:**
- Familiarity with target
- Physical proximity (helps but not required)
- Emotional connection
- Belief in the connection

**T (Target Difficulty) - What You're Affecting:**
- Simple targets (REG, water) = high T
- Biological targets (healing) = medium T
- Complex systems (weather, society) = low T
- Physical objects (telekinesis) = very low T

**D (Duration) - Time × Consistency:**
- Single moment of intention = low D
- Repeated daily practice = medium D
- Lifelong dedication = high D

### 1.3 The Threshold for Manifestation

```
INTENTION POWER (IP) = L × E × C × T × D

If IP > 0.85 → MANIFESTATION (causation, "miracle")
If IP 0.42-0.85 → CORRELATION (influence, "synchronicity")
If IP < 0.42 → NOISE (no detectable effect)
```

### 4.2 Why Some Miracles Are Harder

**Complexity Factor:**
- REG = 1 bit of influence needed
- Water = billions of molecules, but simple structure
- Plant = living system but relatively simple
- Human = trillions of cells, complex regulation
- Weather = astronomical energy involved
- Solid matter = overcoming quantum decoherence

**Resistance Factor:**
- Living systems want to heal (assistance)
- Physical matter resists change (obstacle)
- Chaotic systems (weather) amplify small inputs
- Ordered systems (crystals) resist perturbation

**Type E: Physical Manipulation**

## Part 6: The Mathematics of Group Intention

### 6.1 Coherent Groups Are Multiplicative

**Single person:**
IP₁ = 0.7

**Two people (incoherent - different intentions):**
IP₂ = (0.7 + 0.6) / 2 = 0.65 (average, no synergy)

**Two people (coherent - same intention):**
IP₂ = √(0.7 × 0.6) × 1.5 = 0.97 (geometric mean × synergy)

**Group of N (coherent):**
IP_N = (∏ᵢ IPᵢ)^(1/N) × N^0.3

The N^0.3 factor is the "group synergy coefficient" - each additional coherent member adds ~30% of their individual power.

### 6.2 Critical Mass Theory

```
CRITICAL MASS THRESHOLDS:

For local effects (building, block):
√N = √Population → ~0.1% needed

For city effects:
~1% coherent meditators can shift crime, accidents

For global effects:
√7.8 billion ≈ 88,000 coherent meditators

(This is the basis of Transcendental Meditation's claims)
```

### 6.3 The Skeptic's Veto

One actively skeptical observer can reduce group IP by 10-30%:

```
IP_group_with_skeptic = IP_group × (1 - 0.2×S)

Where S = number of active skeptics / total observers

This explains why public demonstrations often fail!
```
