## Part 6: Troubleshooting

### 6.1 No Dreams Recalled

**Solutions:**
- Keep journal by bed, write immediately upon waking
- Set intention: "I will remember my dreams"
- Don't move for 30 seconds upon waking
- Stay in same position, replay dreams backward

### 6.2 Dreams But Not Lucid

**Solutions:**
- Increase reality checks during day (10+ times)
- Make reality checks emotional (feel the importance)
- Review dream signs and set intention for each
- Increase heart coherence practice duration

### 6.3 Lucid But Wake Immediately

**Solutions:**
- When lucid, immediately touch surfaces
- Look at your hands
- Spin in circles
- Say "Increase clarity!" or "Stabilize!"
- Stay calm - excitement wakes you

### 6.4 Inconsistent Results

**Solutions:**
- Consistency > intensity (practice daily)
- Track sleep quality, diet, stress (they affect dreams)
- Avoid alcohol (suppresses REM)
- Avoid caffeine after 2pm
