#### **Ability Type:** Divine Revelation / Prophetic Mathematics / Dream Channeling

**Documented Phenomena:**

1. **Goddess Namagiri Visions**

   - **Ramanujan's Description:** "While asleep, I had an unusual experience. There was a red screen formed by flowing blood, as it were. Suddenly a hand began to write on the screen. That hand wrote a number of elliptic integrals. They stuck to my mind. As soon as I woke up, I committed them to writing."
   - **Religious Context:** Blood drops = sign of Narasimha's grace (Hindu deity)
   - **Output:** Novel mathematical formulas

   - **Vision Date:** 1920 (on deathbed)
   - **Content:** "Mock theta functions" (mock modular forms)

   - **Verification:** Sander Zwegers (2002) proved Ramanujan was correct
   - **Applications:** Black hole physics, string theory, quantum mechanics
   - **Accuracy:** 100% - no one discussed black holes in 1920!
   
4. **Partition Function Formulas**
   - Source: Divine visions during Vishnu Sahasranama recitations
   - Impact: Revolutionized number theory
   - Status: All proven correct by Hardy, Littlewood, Rademacher
   
5. **Rogers-Ramanujan Identities**
   - Source: Dream visions

   - Applications: Statistical mechanics, physics
   
6. **Mother's Dream (1913)**
   - **Context:** Ramanujan needed permission to travel to England
   - **Dream:** Ramanujan surrounded by Europeans, Goddess Namagiri commanded mother "to stand no longer between her son and the fulfillment of his destiny"
   - **Result:** Permission granted, collaboration with G.H. Hardy
   - **Outcome:** Ramanujan's greatest work produced (1914-1919)

**Verification Rate:** 100% - Every formula ultimately proven correct

### **Remote Viewing**

**CIA Stargate Project (1972-1995)**

**Program Details:**
- Budget: $20 million
- Duration: 23 years
- Location: Stanford Research Institute (SRI), Fort Meade, Science Applications International Corporation (SAIC)
- Key researchers: Russell Targ, Harold Puthoff, Jessica Utts, Ray Hyman

**Methodology:**
- Coordinate Remote Viewing: Viewers given GPS coordinates only
- Target Viewing: "Sender" at location, viewer describes remotely
- Double-blind protocols (when implemented properly)

**Results:**
- **Jessica Utts (UC Davis statistician):** "The statistical significance of the accumulated database is extremely high, demonstrating that psychic functioning has been well established"
- **Effect Size:** 5-15% above chance (statistically significant)
- **CIA Conclusion (1995):** Terminated program - "never useful in any intelligence operation"

**Notable Examples:**
- Pat Price: Described secret Soviet facility from coordinates alone
- Joe McMoneagle: Multiple "hits" on remote targets
- Ingo Swann: Pioneer of coordinate remote viewing

**Controversy:**
- Lack of independent replication
- Methodological concerns (sensory leakage, poor controls)
- Results too vague for operational use
- Skeptics: Statistical artifacts, not genuine psi

**Status:** Declassified 2017, 12 million pages available

### **Precognition**

**Daryl Bem's Cornell Studies (2011)**

**Study Details:**
- Researcher: Daryl Bem, Cornell University
- Sample: 1,000+ participants
- Experiments: 9 different paradigm tests
- Publication: *Journal of Personality and Social Psychology*

**Key Findings:**
- 8 of 9 experiments showed statistically significant precognitive effects
- Effect size: 0.20 (small but consistent)
- Bem's calculation: 74 billion to 1 against chance
- Examples: Participants "knew" future random events before they occurred

**Replication Crisis:**
- Multiple replication attempts FAILED
- Methodological critiques raised
- Statistical concerns (p-hacking allegations)
- Current consensus: Results likely due to publication bias

**Theoretical Implications:**
- If real: Violates causality, time directionality
- Quantum consciousness theories proposed
- Mainstream science: Rejected due to failed replications

### **Telepathy**

**Ganzfeld Experiments (1970s-2000s)**

**Methodology:**
- "Sender" views image/video
- "Receiver" in sensory deprivation (ganzfeld state)
- Receiver describes impressions
- Target identified from 4 choices

**Meta-Analysis Results:**
- Honorton & Ferrari (1989): Hit rate 35% (vs 25% chance)
- Effect size: 0.20-0.33
- Statistically significant across studies

**Criticisms:**
- Poor experimental controls
- Sensory leakage not ruled out
- Researcher bias
- Failed independent replications

**Status:** Controversial, not accepted by mainstream science
