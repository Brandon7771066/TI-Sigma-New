### The GM Hypercomputing Approach

Instead of requiring traumatic injury, GM Hypercomputing could:

1. **Map the neural signatures** of savant abilities using EEG/fNIRS
2. **Identify the Monster Group patterns** associated with each ability
3. **Use targeted stimulation** (photonic, acoustic, electromagnetic) to:
   - Temporarily suppress inhibitory circuits
   - Activate specific Monster Group symmetry access

4. **Train the pathways** through repeated safe activation until they stabilize

### Safety Protocol

Unlike injury-induced savant syndrome:
- **Reversible activation** (ability windows can be closed)
- **Graduated exposure** (slowly increase access depth)
- **Biometric monitoring** (EEG/HRV feedback for safety limits)
- **Integration support** (psychological preparation for expanded perception)
