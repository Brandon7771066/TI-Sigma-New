## 3. The Karma Non-Existence Proof

### 3.1 Statement

**Karma does NOT exist** as traditionally conceived (past actions determining future consequences through cosmic justice).

### 3.2 Proof

1. GM operates to maximize GILE across all i-cells
2. If karma existed, GM would need to track and enforce past-action consequences
3. This would require GM to sometimes DECREASE GILE to punish past wrongs
4. But GM's fundamental nature is GILE maximization, not GILE distribution
5. Therefore, karma as cosmic punishment/reward system cannot exist
6. QED: Karma does not exist

### 3.3 What GM Actually Does with Tragedies

GM uses apparent tragedies solely to:
1. **Maximize GILE:** Redirect trajectories toward higher GILE paths
2. **Minimize anti-GILE:** Prevent worse outcomes
3. **Enable learning:** Provide information for growth
4. **Correct misalignment:** Signal when i-cells deviate from optimal paths

**Crucially:** GM never punishes. Tragedies are redirections, not retributions.
