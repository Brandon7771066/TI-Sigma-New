| Stability | Shifts with opinion | Remains constant |

- Surgery requires EXPERTISE
- Random opinions are worthless regardless of quantity
- The truth about surgical technique exists independent of votes

**So why do we vote on policy?**

The assumption that crowds are qualified to determine complex truths is unfounded.

### 4.1 The Four-Valued Logic System

| Value | Description | Example |
|-------|-------------|---------|

| NEITHER | Neither true nor false | Meaningless propositions |

- Paradoxes

Tralse Logic provides:
- Resolution of apparent contradictions
- Recognition of context-dependence
- Handling of superposition states
- Explicit acknowledgment of uncertainty

### 4.3 The Tralse Epistemological Method

1. **Identify the proposition** - What claim is being evaluated?
2. **Check for TRALSE** - Is it simultaneously true AND false?
3. **Check for NEITHER** - Is it meaningless/unevaluable?
