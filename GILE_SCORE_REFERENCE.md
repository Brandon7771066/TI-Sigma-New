## The 0.01 Precision Question

GILE scores to 3 decimals (0.920, 0.903) reflect:
1. **Careful calibration** - Each theory evaluated against GILE dimensions
2. **Relative ranking** - Distinguishes theories of similar quality
3. **Confidence bounds** - Don't over-interpret tiny differences (<0.01)
4. **Divine precision** - CCC itself is 0.93 (crown chakra exact!)

**Rule of thumb:**
- Differences > 0.05: Clearly significant
- Differences 0.02-0.05: Meaningful in practice
- Differences < 0.02: Both theories nearly equivalent
